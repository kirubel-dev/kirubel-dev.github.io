About Yirgacheffe Coffee

Yirgacheffe is a coffee region in southern Ethiopia that produces distinctively floral and fruit-toned coffees from traditional Ethiopian varieties of Arabica long grown in the region. Yirgacheffe coffees are famous for their bright acidity and complex flavor profiles.

Our beans are sourced directly from small-scale farmers and cooperatives in the Yirgacheffe region, ensuring fair compensation for the growers and the highest quality product for our customers.

Tasting Notes:

  • Bright, clean acidity
  • Floral aroma with notes of jasmine
  • Fruity undertones of citrus and berry
  • Medium body with a sweet, clean finish

Brewing Recommendations

To fully experience the complex flavors of Yirgacheffe coffee, we recommend the following brewing methods:

1
Pour Over

Highlights the bright acidity and floral notes. Use medium-fine grind.

2
AeroPress

Creates a clean cup with pronounced fruity notes. Use fine grind.

3
Traditional Ethiopian Coffee Ceremony

For an authentic experience, use a jebena pot and serve with popcorn or roasted barley.
